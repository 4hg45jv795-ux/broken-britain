EnoughIs Enough

Britain's gone to the dogs.

A side-scrolling brawl through a country that's lost the plot. Explore the town hub — church, gun shop, library, the Winchester, cinema, airport and station — kit yourself out, then take the portal to the streets and battle on through Belfast, Dundee, Glasgow and beyond. Buy guns at FAFO Ammo & Arms, summon mates like the Hurler and the flaming wheelie-bin, and keep an eye on the skies. Pick your fighter to drop in.

On a phone? Turn it sideways — the game goes full screen in landscape.

The Hurler — charges in and takes out the two nearest enemies, then legs it. Tap his button mid-fight to summon him; he needs a breather before you can call him again.
Fire Bin — a flaming wheelie-bin rolls right across the screen, wiping out everyone in its path. Tap to unleash it; longer cooldown than the Hurler.

Tap a fighter to drop into the world.
